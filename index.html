Mvuli Lodge

Mvuli Lodge – Ngorongoro

Experience Safari Luxury

Discover a boutique luxury safari lodge nestled near the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Located in the charming town of Karatu, our lodge serves as the perfect gateway to unforgettable safari experiences in northern Tanzania.

Each of our elegantly appointed suites offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, combining modern luxury with authentic African design. Wake up to the sounds of nature, enjoy gourmet dining under the stars, and embark on thrilling game drives through one of Africa's most remarkable ecosystems.

Mvuli Hotel

Mvuli Hotel Arusha

Serenity in the City

Mvuli Hotel provides a serene countryside experience within the city. Surrounded by lush gardens, a beautiful tree plantation, and a crystal-clear stream flowing year-round from nearby mountains, our Arusha property offers a tranquil escape from the bustle of city life.

With 18 spacious rooms featuring private balconies overlooking the verdant grounds, you'll find the perfect balance of comfort and convenience. Whether you are visiting for business or leisure, our dedicated team ensures an unforgettable stay with warm Tanzanian hospitality.
